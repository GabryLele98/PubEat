FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

1. The system shall provide a functionality to rate(1) pub services(2).

2. The system shall provide a functionality to:
	a. create a matching (3);
	b. share the created matching(3) to the pub showcase.

3. The system shall provide a system of filters(4) for the pubs research.


DICTIONARY:

rate(1): users can rate the pub services by choosing stars in a range of 1 -:- 5 stars.
services(2): menu, service, location, price.
matching(3): this refers to a matching between food and drink.
filters(4): filters are by style, position, ratings, type of foods & drinks
